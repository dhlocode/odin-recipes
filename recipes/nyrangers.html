New York Rangers

New York deli style hot sandwich on a hero roll

Deli sandwich piled high with sliced chicken breast, turkey, cheese, and all the toppings. Served hot on a hero roll, kaiser roll, rye, sourdough, or wheat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Turkey breast
  • Cheddar
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Sliced tomato
  • Red onion
  • BBQ sauce
  • Butter
  • Mayo
  • Hero roll or brioche bun

Directions

  1. Toast the roll or bun
  2. Add butter on one side
  3. Add mayo on the other side
  4. Heat chicken and turkey in pan or microwave. Add to the sandwich.
  5. Add cheddar cheese
  6. Add shredded lettuce
  7. Add sliced tomato
  8. Top with BBQ sauce
