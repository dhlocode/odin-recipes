Crispy Chicken Burritos

Crispy fried burritos filled with chicken and cheese

Pacific northwest-inspired burritos filled with chicken and cheese mixture with a flaky crispy shell.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Ground chicken breast
  • Cream cheese
  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped green chilis
  • Smoked paprika
  • Oil for frying

Directions

  1. Cook the chicken in a pan
  2. Chop the onion into fine pieces
  3. Add chicken, onion, green chilis, and cream cheese and mix well.
  4. Add 1/2 tsp smoked paprika to taste.
  5. Roll mixture into long tortillas
  6. Fry burritos in deep dish pan with hot oil until golden brown
