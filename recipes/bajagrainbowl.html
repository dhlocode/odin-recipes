Baja Grain Bowl

Mediterranean style baja bowl

Warm multi-grain baja bowl served with chicken and Greek ingredients with a Mexican flair

Ingredients

  • Sliced grilled chicken breast
  • Brown rice
  • Quinoa blend
  • Lime juice
  • Cilantro
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Black bean and corn salsa
  • Pickled red onions
  • Avocados
  • Red grape tomatoes
  • Salsa verde
  • Plain Greek yogurt
  • Feta cheese crumbles

Directions

  1. Add rice and quinoa to a bowl. Mix in lime juice, olive oil, and cilantro.
  2. Add black bean and corn salsa
  3. Add red onions
  4. Add sliced avocados
  5. Add sliced red grape tomatoes
  6. Add salsa verde
  7. Add a dollup of Greek yogurt
  8. Sprinkle with feta cheese crumbles
